ABOUT

For the last six years I have been working as a graphic designer. I love art and music. I graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree in Visual Communications from Weber State University. For the last 3 and a half years I have been working for the LDS Church. I have grown tremendously as a designer in my time here. I love to collaborate. I have a positive and laid back personality and feel like I can help bring a team together. I strive to create designs that accomplish the job they need to do. I hope to find a longterm position where the work I do benefits me and my employer but also helps to improve the lives of others.
